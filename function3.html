Manage Your Inventory Effectively

The Inventory Management function allows users to efficiently manage their entire stock of medicines. This includes adding new medicines, updating quantities, and removing expired items. Users can categorize medicines for better organization and easy access.

Key Features:

  • Add new medicines with details such as name, type, quantity, and expiry date.
  • Update existing medicine quantities as they are used or received.
  • Remove expired medicines from the inventory to maintain safety and compliance.
  • Organize medicines into categories for easier navigation.

Benefits:

  • Streamlines the process of managing medicine stock.
  • Provides insights into inventory levels and trends.
  • Facilitates better decision-making regarding reordering and stock levels.
