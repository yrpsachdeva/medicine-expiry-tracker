Overview of Key Functions

The Medicines Expiry Tracker is designed to help pharmacies and individuals manage their medicine inventory effectively. Below are the key functions that make this application essential for maintaining a safe and efficient pharmacy environment.

1. Track Expiry Dates

This function allows users to input the expiry dates of all medicines stored in their inventory. The application will automatically track these dates and provide alerts as the expiry date approaches.

Benefits:

  • Reduces the risk of using expired medicines.
  • Helps maintain compliance with health regulations.
  • Improves inventory management efficiency.

2. Notifications and Alerts

Receive timely notifications via email or SMS when medicines are nearing their expiry dates. Users can customize the alert settings based on their preferences.

Benefits:

  • Ensures timely action is taken to use or dispose of medicines.
  • Reduces waste and saves costs on expired products.
  • Enhances user awareness of inventory status.

3. Inventory Management

This function allows users to manage their entire inventory, including adding new medicines, updating quantities, and removing expired items. Users can also categorize medicines for better organization.

Benefits:

  • Streamlines the process of managing medicine stock.
  • Provides insights into inventory levels and trends.
  • Facilitates better decision-making regarding reordering and stock levels.

4. Reporting and Analytics

Generate detailed reports on medicine usage, expiry trends, and inventory levels. This function helps users analyze their inventory data to make informed decisions.

Benefits:

  • Identifies patterns in medicine usage.
  • Helps in forecasting future inventory needs.
  • Supports compliance with regulatory reporting requirements.
